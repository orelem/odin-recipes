Cheesecake Stuffed Strawberries

Description

These strawberries are bursting with the delicious flavor of cheesecake. Each bite is a juicy and creamy treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth and leave you craving more. Perfect for a summer dessert or a special occasion, these cheesecake-stuffed strawberries are easy to make and can be customized with your favorite flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and cored
  • 3 graham crackers, crushed, or as needed

Steps

  1. Mix together cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla extract in a bowl. Fill a piping bag or resealable bag with the mixture.
  2. Core the strawberries and fill the cavities with the cream cheese mixture. Dip the filled side of the strawberries in crushed graham crackers to coat them.
