Lemon Cookies

Description

This recipe makes a batch of tasty, soft cookies that are flavored with lemon juice and zest. They are easy to make and only require a few lemons to give them a delicious citrus flavor. These cookies will be a hit with the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon zest
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup confectioners' sugar for rolling

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat together 1 cup of sugar, 1 cup of butter, and the zest of one lemon using an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together 2 eggs, the juice of one lemon, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Beat this mixture into the butter mixture on low speed.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 3/4 cups of flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Gradually add this flour mixture to the egg mixture on low speed until just combined. Cover the dough and chill it in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  5. Form the dough into balls using a cookie scoop or tablespoon and roll each ball in confectioners' sugar. Place the balls 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheets and lightly press down on each one with your hand.
  6. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven until the centers are just set, about 11-12 minutes. Let the cookies cool on a wire rack before storing them in an airtight container.
